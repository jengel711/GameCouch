Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

Can I come in and test out a game?

Yes, we have a wide assortment of games to come in and test out or just rent-to-play. You can come in and pick a game from our selection or reserve a copy through our online reservation system to have waiting for you at your table.

Can we reserve a table to play at?

Yes, you can reserve a table using our online system or by booking with one of our valued NPCs.

How do I reserve a table?

Use our online portal or speak with one of our valued NPCs to book a space for your party to play.

What is the return policy?

GameCouch offers a 60 day return policy for most items. Proof of purchase such as a receipt or order confirmation number is required.

Outside drinks are allowed as long as they are in a spill proof container. We do ask that you leave outside food at home.
